Biography

I’m a research assistant in the School of Health and Wellbeing at the University of Glasgow. My work focuses on using real-world data and causal inference models to inform health policy and practice.
I also teach in the School of Medicine at Universidad Científica del Sur in Peru.

Interests

  • Causal inference
  • Epidemiology of NCDs
  • Real-world evidence and disease modelling
  • Global health
